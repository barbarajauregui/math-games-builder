## §3 — Phase 1 list (under-covered fixes)

Audit 13 §4 listed 7 coverage-gap standards in scope (K.OA.A.3, K.OA.A.4, K.CC.A.1, K.CC.A.2, K.CC.A.3 — plus the two 3.OA gaps which are out of Phase 1 scope here). Re-scanning the JSON against the STRONG threshold (≥3 PRIMARY) found 3 more under-covered in-scope standards Audit 13 did not flag in its priority table: K.CC.B.4b (1 PRIMARY), K.CC.B.4c (2 PRIMARY), K.CC.C.7 (2 PRIMARY). That brings the Phase 1 list to **8 standards**.

Verbatim Phase 1 list:

1. **K.OA.A.3** — 1 PRIMARY (`cuisenaire-rods`). Needs multiplicity-enforcement fix on `number-frames-decompose`, `split-the-loot`, `number-bonds`.
2. **K.OA.A.4** — 2 PRIMARY (`number-frames`, `cuisenaire-rods`). Needs partner-of-10 specialization (Mechanic Inventor).
3. **K.CC.A.1** — 0 PRIMARY. Needs count-to-100 recitation/production engine.
4. **K.CC.A.2** — 0 PRIMARY. Needs counting-on-from-N engine; `number-frames` REVISABLE fix listed.
5. **K.CC.A.3** — 0 PRIMARY. Needs numeral-tracing engine.
6. **K.CC.B.4b** — 1 PRIMARY (`free-collect` only carries order-irrelevance). Bring `number-frames` or `cuisenaire-rods` to PRIMARY by enforcing varied starting arrangements.
7. **K.CC.B.4c** — 2 PRIMARY (`number-frames`, `free-collect`). One more PRIMARY needed (promote `cuisenaire-rods` swap-rod-for-one-longer to PRIMARY framing).
8. **K.CC.C.7** — 2 PRIMARY (`sorting-lane`, `leaderboard-fix`). One more numeral-compare engine needed (consider promoting `size-picker` once K-specialization confirmed).

Note: K.CC.A.1/A.2/A.3 sit at ZERO PRIMARY. They are grouped under Phase 1 because entries exist in the map (with REVISABLE notes) and the audit treats them as "under-covered" rather than "missing." The brief's strict definition would mark them UNDER_COVERED only if they had 1–2 PRIMARY engines — they have 0, but they are not UNMAPPED either.
